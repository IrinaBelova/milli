What we do…

If you've ever written a letter and then not sent it because you didn't find time to get to the post office. Or though "I must write and thank them for that" but not done it because of the dificulty of getting paper, a pen, an envelope and a stamp all in the same place, at the same time. If this sounds like you then posted notes is here to help.

We take your text and turn it into beautiful, handwritten notes. We put a first class stamp on it and post it.

The Rules

We'd love to spend all day writing thank you cards for your grandma, love letters, anonymous high five letters and other feel good things. We don't mind if you get us to write letters to your landlord or pleas for the library to let you off your fines. We'll even politely fire all your staff or tell someone how terribly rude they are. We won't send death threats or Ransom notes.
